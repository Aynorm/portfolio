/FAQS

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a website?

The timeline depends on the size and complexity of the project.

  • ✧ Basic website: 1–3 weeks
  • ✧ Business or service site: 3–6 weeks
  • ✧ E-commerce or custom site: 6–12+ weeks

    • We’ll provide a project timeline after understanding your specific needs.

How much does a website cost?

Pricing varies based on features, design complexity, and content.

  • ✧ Basic website: $100–$1,000
  • ✧ Advanced or custom site: $1,000–$10,000+

    • We offer a detailed quote after a free consultation.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

Yes. Every website we build is fully responsive, meaning it will look and work great on phones, tablets, and desktops.

Can I update the website myself after it's built?

Yes. We typically build with user-friendly content management systems (CMS) like WordPress or Webflow. We also offer training or documentation so you can update content easily.

What if I need help after the website is launched?

We offer ongoing support and maintenance packages. This includes updates, security checks, backups, and content changes.

What do you need from me to get started?

Typically, we’ll need:

  • ✧ Your logo and brand materials
  • ✧ Content (text, images, videos)
  • ✧ Goals for the site (e.g., sales, bookings)
  • ✧ Examples of sites you like

    • We’ll guide you step-by-step to make it easy.

Can you integrate third-party tools like payment systems, booking, or email marketing?

Yes. We can integrate tools like:

  • ✧ Payment gateways (PayPal, Stripe, Flutterwave, etc.)
  • ✧ Booking systems
  • ✧ Email marketing tools (Mailchimp, ConvertKit, etc.)
  • ✧ Social media, chat, CRM, analytics, and more.
